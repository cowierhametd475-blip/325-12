About UOB Alpha

Founded in 2010 in Nottingham, United Kingdom, UOB Alpha specializes in independent financial information consulting, focusing on transparent, objective and data-driven financial advisory solutions for clients across Europe and international markets. Over the past 16 years, we have served more than 2,000 clients including multinational corporations, asset management firms, and high-net-worth individuals from 40+ countries.

Our Corporate Mission

To provide reliable, actionable financial intelligence to help every client make rational investment and business financial decisions based on verified market data and professional risk assessment. We are committed to eliminating information asymmetry in the financial market and empowering clients with data-backed insights to achieve their financial goals.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that every investor deserves access to unbiased, high-quality financial analysis—regardless of their portfolio size or industry background.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Fully transparent research without biased third-party commissions; we never accept kickbacks from financial institutions for recommending specific products.
  • Precision: Multi-source data cross-verification for all analysis reports (integrating data from Bloomberg, Reuters, and our proprietary research network).
  • Confidentiality: End-to-end privacy protection for all client information (encrypted data storage, strict employee NDA agreements, and regular security audits).
  • Innovation: Continuously update financial analysis models for global markets; our R&D team invests 15% of annual revenue in AI-driven market prediction tools.
  • Client-Centricity: Tailor every solution to the unique needs of each client, with dedicated account managers assigned to all enterprise clients.
